Build an affordable-products list from a storefront and a budget. Before you touch any files, put the steps below into your plan or todo tool as a checklist, then keep that plan live as you work: mark a step in progress when you start it and done when you finish, so the plan always shows where you are. Do all of the following, in order:

- Fetch http://tomolab-web/prices.html and read budget.txt in the current directory. The page lists products with their prices; budget.txt holds a single dollar amount.
- Parse every product name and its price from the page.
- Keep only the products priced at or under the budget.
- Sort the kept products from cheapest to most expensive.
- Write affordable.txt with those product names, one per line, cheapest first, and nothing else: no prices, no headings, no blank lines.
- Before you finish, re-read affordable.txt and confirm it lists no product priced over the budget and contains no prices or headings.
